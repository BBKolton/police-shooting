Police Shootings

A map of casualities to police action in the line of duty

About this Map

This is a map of reported police on civilian shootings in America, aggregated by the site DeadSpin. The data is ordered by the reported race of the individual. Shootings that ended in the victim's death are marked red, while non-deaths and unknown outcomes are marked in blue.
